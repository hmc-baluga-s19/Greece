Exploring Greece

Every island is surrounded by beautiful beaches but if you get sick of the sun here are some great alternatives to lounging around.

Wine tasting

Explore your options

Santorini would be the ideal island to venture into
the world of wine. It is very well known for the
wineries in town. There are many varieties of tours
offered. Some involve extensive exploration of the
wineries and teach you a lot about wine. Others are
much shorter and more relaxed. Some tours even
combine a cooking class with wine tasting.

Air and Helicopter Tours

Explore your options

There are many options for helicopter sightseeing over
the islands. This is a great way to fully taken in the
beauty that Greece offers.

Sailing and Water Tours

Explore your options

The best part of any place in Greece is the crystal
clear water. You can scuba dive into the deep blue and
explore under the sea and meet some cute new friends.
It is also fun to sail on the water and just let the beauty
of everything around you sink in.
