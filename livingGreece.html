Living in Greece

There are many islands in and surrounding Greece. This page is meant to help guide your decisions on where to lodge for the duration of your trip.

There are direct flights and ferries from Athens to most of the other islands. However, traveling island to island can be a little more difficult so make sure you plane well. The country's largest ferry port is Piraeus.

Mykonos, Greece

Best Greek island for luxury




Mykonos is known for its nightlife and social scene. They also have beautiful beaches
and beach clubs to occupy yourself during the days before going out on the town at night.
There are a lot of trendy shops and great restaurants in the area. The capital village,
Mykonos Town, is also a very charming area.








Santorini, Greece

Best Greek island for teens

Best Greek island for history

Best Greek island for hiking

Best Greek island for luxury




Santorini is known for breathtaking views, spectacular hikes, delicious food, and exquisite
wine and wineries. This island also is famous for the on site volcano. There are frequent
tours given of the inactive volcano. You will never be bored on this island. There are
neverending beer tours, wine tours and other great things to do.










Crete, Greece

Book your stay!

Best Greek island for beaches

Best Greek island for history

Best Greek island for hiking


Crete is the largest island in Greece and also has the most diverse activities offered.
There are some beautiful, quaint towns you can walk through but there are also some gorgeous
beaches you can lounge on. Crete also has a very rich history and delicious local food.







Ios, Greece

Book your stay!

Best Greek island for singles

Best Greek island for beaches


Ios has a very lively nightlife and great restaurants. It is also surrounded by very
idyllic Greek beaches. It is located very close to Santorini, the two island are only
a 50 minute ferry ride apart.







Naxos, Greece

Book your stay!

Best Greek island for beaches

Best Greek island for hiking


Naxos is notorious for having the best beaches in Greece a long with amazing hikes. It is also
the most family-friendly island in Greece. It is full of history and great local restaurants that
can occupy your days here. The inland villages are also very fun to explore.







Paros, Greece

Book your stay!

Best Greek island for teens


Paros is a mix of Naxos, gorgeous beaches with nice inland villages, and Mykonos, a friendly
party vibe. There are two specific towns in Paros that are filled with nightlife, shopping,
great restaurants and boutique hotels.










Rhodes, Greece

Book your stay!

Best Greek island for teens

Best Greek island for history


Rhodes is one of the best islands for historical sightseeing. The famous Knights Grand Master Palace,
Castello, is in Rhodes Town and is a must-visit museum. The entire vibe of the island is very unique
and makes you feel like you're traveling back into medieval times.




Santorini, Mykonos, Naxos and Paros are in the same island group.
